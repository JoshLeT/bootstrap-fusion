DJs

Josh has practically lived here since he joined us in 2013! He says he doesn't have a favourite song, but we are convinced it's 'Show Me Love' - Robin S, it wouldn't be a Saturday night without it! Josh works on a lot of the stuff behind the scenes here as well. If you want check out his music you can on Spotify

Tammo

Tammo joined us ages ago, back in 2010! If you like a great mashup then Tammo is your man, he's got hundreds of them! He's a true techno head at heart. It would be a rare sight to see him without a Corona in the DJ booth.

Nick

Nick joined us at the end of 2014 as a member of the security team, but you're more likely to see him in the DJ booth these days. His favourite song is the mashup up of We No Speak Americano vs Temperature (Sean Paul) and if you want to buy him a drink, make it a Fireball!


Tru-Luv

Tru-Luv is our resident king of Hip-Hop and R&B since he joined us in 2015 all the way from Ayai Napa, Cyprus! Sean Paul is without a doubt Tru-Luv's favourite artist, you'd think he was ill if you didn't hear him playing any of his songs! He loves a cheeky shot of Sambuca... or three!

Tom C

Since 2012 Tom C has been our resident future and deep house specialist. Despite his love for the genre it would be a strange night if he didn't play 'One' - Swedish House Mafia, he bloody loves that song!
